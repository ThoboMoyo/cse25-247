About Our Page

Thobo Isaac Moyo (ipm) (Born on the 8 of April, 2008) is a producer based in Botswana. He's known for his mix of sounds and cultures which are reflected in his beats; He started his career in 2024 posting his beats on YouTube . In the last two years he's been recognized for the production of the tracks "Losing My Mind” and “Soul Cream” both of them with more than 2k views on YouTube. " I consider that my beats have the cultural mixture I've experienced throughout my life, I was born in Botswana, Gaborone (A Southern African region) listening to rap music, pop and gospel, thanks to the internet I got to know a broader variety of music like hip-hop...that's it. Hip-hop is a bridge to express my form of feeling music" Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/thobo.moyo/ Youtube Channel: https://www.youtube.com/c/prodbyipm
